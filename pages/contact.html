IMP Coordinating Office — Clarksville, TN

⭐ National Headquarters · Manned since 2024

IMP's national coordinating office in Clarksville, Tennessee is the operational command center for the entire IMP Worldwide network. From here, Cleveland Rhodes (Co-Founder, President & CTO) leads network strategy, partnerships, broadcast engineering oversight, and creator-success operations.

What runs from Clarksville:

  • Network operations & engineering command (AzuraCast, Viloud, Bunny.net)
  • Partnerships desk (studios, ad networks, distributors)
  • Creator success & onboarding
  • Editorial & content production
  • Compliance & brand-safety operations

Sits within driving distance of Nashville and the broader Middle Tennessee media corridor.
